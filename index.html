About COMPPEC


COMPPEC is a national level, annual Computer Project Exhibition and Competition which the department of computer engineering at NUST College of E&ME has been hosting for more than a decade now. It is one of the most prestigious computers and technological events held in Pakistan that provides a platform for head to head competition of the brilliant brains from all over the Country in the fields of computer software, hardware, communication and digital technology.


Projects You Can Bring


Electromechanical System

It's now time for all of us to embrace machine learning involving an amalgam of mechanical and electrical systems. This will eventually contribute to the world of mechanics, where your single effort can drastically speed up the pace of electromechanics. Projects related to electrical or mechanical systems with digital logic, microprocessor or microcontroller are welcome in this category. Electromechanical systems based on sensors and actuators can also be included.

Embeded System

Your tiny projects embedded in human needs can surely bring a radical change in a world of technology. Projects containing microcontrolers, microprocessors, FPGA, as its brain are included. Moreover, projects related to communication technology and artificial intelligence or automated technology fall in this category.

Software System

Withstanding 21st century, lets contribute to the phase of mobilehood by letting our creativities shine. Projects including desktop applications and games, virtual reality, augmented reality based projects, mobile apps and games, android or ios, and web based applications and games fall in this category.
